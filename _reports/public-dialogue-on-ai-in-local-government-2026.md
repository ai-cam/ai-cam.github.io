This report presents the full findings from ai@cam's public dialogue on AI in local government, drawing on two phases of engagement with Cambridgeshire residents conducted in November 2025 and March 2026.

The dialogues were designed and delivered by [Hopkins Van Mil](https://www.hopkinsvanmil.co.uk/) and explored public expectations, hopes, and concerns about the role of AI in local public services.

The findings continue to inform ai@cam's [Local Government AI Accelerator](https://www.ai.cam.ac.uk/calls/local-government-ai-accelerator) and offer a framework for councils seeking to deploy AI in ways that earn and maintain public trust.
