biography: Umang Bhatt is an Assistant Professor in Trustworthy AI at CHIA. He
  is also a Fellow in Computer Science at King’s College, Cambridge. Previously,
  Umang was an Assistant Professor & Faculty Fellow at the Center for Data
  Science at New York University and a Senior Research Associate in Safe and
  Ethical AI at the Alan Turing Institute. He has also served as a Research
  Fellow at the Partnership on AI, a Summer Fellow at Harvard’s Center for
  Research on Computation and Society, and an Advisor to the Responsible AI
  Institute. Umang completed his PhD in the Machine Learning Group in the
  Department of Engineering at the University of Cambridge. He also holds an MS
  and BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University.
